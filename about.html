Our Story

Shell Station on Uhuru Highway opened in 2010, when the Kamau family took over the franchise with one clear goal: give Nairobi drivers a station they could trust.

We started with 4 pumps and a small kiosk. Over 15 years, we've grown into a full service station — 6 pumps, a Shell Select convenience store with hot food, a car wash, and a mechanic bay.

What hasn't changed is our commitment: genuine Shell fuel, correct measurements, no queue games, and staff who actually help you.

We live in this city too. We know what it's like to be stuck on Uhuru Highway at 6AM. That's why we open early, close late, and keep prices as competitive as the EPR allows.

🎯 Our Mission

To be Nairobi's most trusted fuel stop — where every litre is genuine, every price is fair, and every customer leaves faster than they arrived.

👁️ Our Vision

A Kenya where every driver has access to quality fuel, honest service, and a hot meal, regardless of how early they hit the road.

🌱 Environment

We use low-sulphur diesel, have solar panels on the canopy, and recycle used engine oil. Every litre sold under Shell's global sustainability standards.

What We Stand For

Six values that guide every decision at this station.

Honesty

Our pumps are calibrated and certified. What you pay for is exactly what you get — every time.

Speed

No unnecessary queues. 6 pumps, trained attendants, and a layout designed to get you back on the road fast.

Safety

CCTV, fire extinguishers, trained staff, clean forecourt, and strict Shell safety protocols at all times.
